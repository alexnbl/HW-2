Don't Forget to Do These Things Before Any Trip

#1 Get a passport and any needed visas.

#2 Get any recommended vaccinations check on the government travel website to find out if any are needed.

#3 Check for any travel-related warnings.

#4 Put a hold on all mail. You don't want a package on your doorstep for a week if you live alone.

#5 Stock up on any travel-size toiletries.

#6 Download any necessary trip apps.

#7 Research any local customs

#8 Order a little bit of foreign currency two weeks in advance from your bank. I prefer to walk off the plane with some cash instead of having to start hunting for an ATM.
